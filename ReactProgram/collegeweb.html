About DHB Soni College

DHB Soni College is a prestigious institution offering a wide range of programs...

MVPM's DHB Soni College established in 2006, is one of the leading educational institutes in the city of Solapur. Over the past decade, the college has earned a prestigious reputation of harnessing a progressive outlook towards education by introducing career oriented courses, ICT enabled teaching facilities, outreach activities and discipline. DHB Soni College offers outstanding graduate programs in B.Sc.(ECS) and BCA , each with a real world focus. These programs equip you with essential workplace skills that are abstract with the needs and aspirations of times. The Junior College of the college was introduced in 2014 which offers courses in science and commerce stream. Under the worthy leadership of the management the faculty and the administrative staff of the DHB Soni College stand shoulder to shoulder to provide our stakeholders a lifetime experience of quality and need based education.
